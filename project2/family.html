PewDiePie's family

PewDiePie's family that we've seen featured in his videos before consists of his wife, Marzia Bisognin, his pugs, Edgar and Maya, and his parents.

Marzia Bisognin

Marzia is Italian Internet personality, fashion designer, businesswoman, ceramics artist, and one-time author. Her once active YouTube channel is now an archive of past videos and she primairly deals with fashion now.

At the end of 2019, she married Felix Kjellberg (PewDiePie) and they have been together since. She had also appeared in numerous videos of his. Here is some information regarding her:

  • Born: October 21, 1992 (age 28 years), Arzignano, Italy
  • Subscribers: 7.62 million
  • Total views: 28.3 million
  • Books: La casa dei sogni
  • Instagram Youtube Twitter

Edgar and Maya

Edgar is PewDiePie's 3rd dog and the one that gets the most attention. He is jokingly named after Edgar Allan Poe (Edgar Allan Pug). Edgar was born in 2012 and has widely been featured as a character in PewDiePie's videos, games and shows. He is a beloved pet to both PewDiePie and his audience.

Maya is PewDiePie's 2nd dog and previously gained most of her attention in Marzia's channel. She is called both Maya and Puga among them. She was born in 2015 and one can notice that she is missing her left eye. This is due to an incident when she was a pup in Vietnam. According to PewDiePie, she has gone blind. Similar to Edgar, she is featured in many of PewDiePie's videos, games and shows.

Johanna Kjellberg and Ulf Kjellberg

There is not too much to say about PewDiePie's parents. They have both been featured in some of PewDiePie's videos but he does not share information about them in his videos and there is not much public information to go on

Ulf Kjellberg is PewDiePie's father. Born: January 8, 1957 (age 64 years).

Johanna Kjellberg is PewDiePie's mother. Born: May 7, 1958 (age 62 years)
