Scientists are currently developing ways for sustainable hydrogen production. The leading development is hydropower which smashes rocks and releases these natural gases. These gases are fed into 0 emission fuel cells for electricity generation. Increased energy efficiency and an increase of the use of renewable energy will reduce the effects of climate change.
Many house amenities contribute to climate change. The efforts of one are not enough, we must all contribute to see the effect. Home users can do the following to contribute:
  • Take shorter showers
  • Turn off the lights when you aren't in the room
  • Use more power efficient light bulbs
  • Install solar panels
  • Wash dishes by hand
  • Eat less meat
  • Plant trees
  • Recycle and compost
As of 2020, the US was ranked #2 in carbon emissions at just over 1/2 of the emissions in Gigatons that China releases. Many of what we do daily contributes to this. The food we eat, the heating we use, the things we buy, and the transportation we use all contribute to global warming.
